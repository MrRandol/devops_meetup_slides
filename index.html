TL;DL in 5 points

Break Silos

Make people work together, communicate Teams are in the center, if they do not understand/want to change, no point

Go iterative

Do not bigbang anything, improve by small chunks Decide to improve one thing in a short period of time, measure the improvement If it fails, it is ok (cf next), just rollback and redo

It is ok to fail

Something not working is not a failure, as long as you can fix it quick Since iterative, easy to rollback and change direction You learn from mistakes, as kids, if we never fall we never learn

Get feedback

2 things : - prove that you are improving - get feedback on the state of the project
